✦ Our Story ✦

Born from Devotion,
Built with Love

Sri Pada was founded in 2020, driven by a heartfelt desire to offer weary pilgrims a comfortable and affordable home away from home near the sacred Tirumala hills.

Named after the divine footprints of Lord Venkateswara, our Residency embodies the spirit of Seva — selfless service. Every room, every meal, and every interaction is an act of devotion to our Lord and our guests.

Over a decade, we have welcomed over 5,000 guests from across India and the world, each one becoming part of our extended family. Our staff are not just employees — they are devotees who take pride in making every stay a blessed experience.
